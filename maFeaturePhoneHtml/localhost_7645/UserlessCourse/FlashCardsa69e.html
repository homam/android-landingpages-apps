Netiquette

Everybody should know proper "Netiquette", Internet etiquette, which is all about sending your electronic messages properly. Here are the rules:

  1. Refrain from personal abuse Never call people names or threaten them in any way.
  2. Don't spam Don't repeatedly post something. Most sites have specific rules about the content, about who is allowed to post comments and the sentiment of that post.
  3. Write clearly Avoid using slang or language specific words. Many non-native speakers might not get your drift.
  4. Be honest Pretending to be someone else is not a smart thing to do. You may chose to be anonymous if that is appropriate but don't make up an identity.
  5. Be aware. You're posts are public They can be read by literally everybody
  6. Research your facts Before posting something or forwarding emails, make sure it is factual.
  7. Do not post material to which you do not own the rights You don't want to face the possibility of legal action.
