Mobile Photography
Start Course

You think you need a good camera to take good pictures. But what if you don't have one, or you don't have it with you but still want to capture the moment? Chances are that you do have a mobile phone camera with you...

The Mobile Photography course teaches you how to take great shots with your mobile camera. It discusses cool gadgets, the technical photography details of ISO, noise reduction and exposure as well as different photography types like outdoor, action, concerts, architecture, still life photography and portraits.

So, go out there and use your phone creatively, rather than for endless texting!
