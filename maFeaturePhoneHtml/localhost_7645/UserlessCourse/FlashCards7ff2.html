Have a meeting

We have meetings with colleagues all the time, Why not have a meeting with your partner? Clear communication is the key. Here are some ground rules to make sure your 'meeting' will not turn into a fight:

  • Do not interrupt your partner.
  • Listen very carefully.
  • Stick to the subject.
  • Ask how your partner felt in certain situations.
  • Recap to make sure you interpreted correctly.
  • Try to find out what triggered the outburst. This will help you find out what may have caused the anger.
  • Do not display non-productive behaviour like: yelling, name calling, using generalisations like "you always", interrupting your partner, bringing up previous issues, manipulating your partner with giving ultimatums.
Positive Note: End your meeting on a positive note. Make a list of things or behaviours you both can change to better the situation. The goal is to clear the air and to make your partner feel loved.
