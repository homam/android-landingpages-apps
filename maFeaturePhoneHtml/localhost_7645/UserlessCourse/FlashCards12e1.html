Don't be dillusional

Stop telling yourself the following:

  • Happiness will come after my great success According to research happiness makes you more productive and successful. Which means you have to turn that thought on its head.
  • Expensive things make me happy It is tempting to say: "if only I had this expensive car, if only I had that designer bag, if only I had this big house, than I would be perfectly happy". But really, the only 'if only' any of us needs is: "if only I could remember that there is no material thing that can perfect my life".
  • Happiness is something you were born with Happiness sometimes takes work. Try to count your blessings. Appreciate the good things in life, connect to the people you care about, help others and think about the bigger picture in life.
