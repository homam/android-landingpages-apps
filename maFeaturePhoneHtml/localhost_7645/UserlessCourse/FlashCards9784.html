E-mail

Send an image to another phone or to your PC in any location via e-mail.

If your images are too large (too many bytes), you can convert the images into smaller ones by using formats supported by multiple computers. For example, JPEG (JPG) and GIF images.

If you want to send larger files or multiple images, you can transfer them via online email services. Check out the following tools:

  • Dropbox.com
  • WeTransfer.com
  • Sendthisfile.com
  • Transferbigfiles.com
