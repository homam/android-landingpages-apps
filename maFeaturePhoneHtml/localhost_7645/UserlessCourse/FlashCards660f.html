Overview

Let's say you are about to marry. You may not be looking forward to negotiating prices with wedding vendors. After all, this is your wedding we're talking about - the best day of your life.

Think of it this way, your goal is to develop a good relationship with all wedding vendors - whether it's the florist, the guy who bakes your wedding cake or your photographer- so that you can get what you really for the price you are prepared to pay.

Here are some strategies to take you through the best day of your life, for the best price.
