Analysing

The purpose of an analysing presentation is to take already existing information and recommend a direction and strategy for the topic.

You might want to deliver a presentation on the expansion plan of your company. For this, it's important that you already have a detailed strategy and analysis of the company's objectives, programs and contingencies, say, for the next year (short term) and the next three years (long term) based on your company's prior performance.
