Overview

It is very easy to misunderstand the culture of other countries. While one country may emphasise humbleness and modesty, another might use an aggressive approach and coercive methods as a form of negotiation. It is also easy to offend people from other cultures without realising you are doing so. This chapter gives some tips and tricks to avoid awkward situations while negotiating with other cultures.
