Committed

The committed section of the audience is the easiest to please. They are mentally prepared to absorb your presentation and take away as much as they can. They generally do not question back, take down notes and have a positive body language. However, just because they are easy to please, you shouldn't take them for granted. Make eye contact with them, acknowledge them and make them a part of your presentation.
