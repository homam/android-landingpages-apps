Emotional abuse

Bruises, broken bones and scratches can be signs of physical abuse. Emotional abuse leaves no obvious marks but is just as harmful as the physical abuse. It is just more subtle.

The first step in the process of ending this cruelty is to recognise the types of emotional abuse:

  1. Unrealistic expectations When your partner makes unreasonable and unreachable expectations which will make you feel like a failure. This can be: expressing criticism and constantly demanding for things or attention.
  2. Verbal abuse Think of: belittling, criticising, name calling, yelling, threatening, blaming and putting the other down in public.
  3. Denial Your partner is constantly denying that certain events took place or certain things he/she said without listening to your point of view.
  4. Emotional Blackmail When your partner threatens to punish you if you don't do what he/she wants.
