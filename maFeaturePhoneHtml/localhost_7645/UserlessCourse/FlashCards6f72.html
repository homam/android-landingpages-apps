Instructional

The main purpose of an instructional presentation is to instruct or direct your audience for a definitive result. These presentations work well when you'd like your audience to gain new knowledge or a skill. Follow these steps:

  1. Convince your audience about the information with clarity.
  2. Demonstrate the process or skill involved.
  3. Show a connection between the topic and its practical usability.
  4. Make it an interactive session with lots of questions and an open forum for feedback and ideas.
