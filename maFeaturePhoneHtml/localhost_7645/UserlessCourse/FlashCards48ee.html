Protective covers

Here are some reasons why it is advisable to use a protective cover:

Drop protection

You will in all probability drop your phone a couple of times during the time span that you will own it. The type of protection it needs against such drops actually depends on the kind of phone you possess. Many Android phones survive them. The iPhone 4 or 4S and the 5 on the other hand are less tough and will suffer more from the occasional drop.

Beyond cosmetics

Many phones such as the iPhone have a camera lens at the back, which means they are more prone to damage. If you drop your phone and get a scratch on the lens, the photos will not look that great.

Resale value

The resale value will considerably drop if you have lots of nicks and scratches on it. Keeping a phone case is the best way to protect your phone from such issues.

Stand out

Have you ever noticed when you are at a party or a social gathering, how many people are carrying the same phone as you do? A phone case can make your phone stand out.
