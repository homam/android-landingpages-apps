Waterproof cases

A phone can be used to take photos during snorkeling, diving, skiing or in very dusty environments using a waterproof case. Some cases are specially designed for the camera phone.

In case you are going to explore the underwater world with your mobile camera, check out the maximum depth of the protective case. Every underwater housing case has its own maximum depth.
