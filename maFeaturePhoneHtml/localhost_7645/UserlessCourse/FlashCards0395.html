Colour

Like fonts, colour also plays a very important role in maintaining legibility and retain audience attention. Have a look at the following points to ensure a good looking presentation.

  • Use a font colour that contrasts sharply with the background; like a blue/black font on a white background and not yellow on a cream background.
  • Use colour to reinforce important points but be careful while applying this rule. Too much usage of different colours is distracting and annoying for your audience.
  • Use a standard colour for your main points and sub-points. Don't use a different colour for every point. It will only serve to distract from the information you are providing.
