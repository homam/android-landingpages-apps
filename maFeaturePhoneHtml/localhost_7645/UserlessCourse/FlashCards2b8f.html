Be passionate

Never let anybody discourage you when thinking about pursuing your dream. Life should be about challenges, happiness and fulfillment. Envision a career that you adore, a hobby that can earn you money, something to leave you feeling content and satisfied. If you do not try, you will never know. Of course you need to be realistic about the money you need to earn but finding a career you can be passionate about can often lead to fulfilling your financial needs as well.
