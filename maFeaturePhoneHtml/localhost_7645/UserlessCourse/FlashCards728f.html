Connect!

We are social animals. We need to connect. Learn to relate to others not just for professional purposes, but for personal reasons as well. While relating you may find out you share common interests with your colleagues and work suddenly does not seem as boring.

Help others when you can. Do not offer help because you are expecting others to return a favour though. Help because you can and are willing.
