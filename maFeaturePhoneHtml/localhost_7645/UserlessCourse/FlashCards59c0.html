3. Read non-verbal cues

No doubt verbal cues are important; but it is equally important to understand non-verbal cues. A negotiator doesn't normally put his entire message into words. His verbal message may express honesty and commitment, while his gestures, facial expressions and voice might say something completely different.

(To know more about non- verbal cues, read the 'Body Language' and the 'Interpreting the Body' chapters)
