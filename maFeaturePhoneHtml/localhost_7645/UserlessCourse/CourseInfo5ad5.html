Learn Arabic
Start Course

Arabic is one of the oldest dialects in the world and is written from right to left. Arabic has lent many words to not only other Middle Eastern and African countries but also to the contemporary English speaking European world as well. This introductory course enables you to use the Modern Standard Arabic in daily life. Learn what to say when you want to greet a local person, order food at a local restaurant or thank a person for his patronage. You just need to select your mother tongue and voilà, there is a completely different language in your repertoire!
