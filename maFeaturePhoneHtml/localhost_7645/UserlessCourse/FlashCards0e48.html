Combine multiple photos

The limitations of a camera phone often leaves you a choice - nicely toned land or a rich textured sky. Compose both the pictures in such a way to keep the frame in exactly the same position. The pictures can now be combined using a photo editing software or app.

Use a tripod to keep the frame in exactly the same position. If you can't use a tripod, lock your arms and legs and pivot your torso to capture all the shots.
