Inspirational

Inspirational presentations make people think and take note of certain issues and situations. It's important that your presentation appeals to both the emotional and intellectual side of your audience. You want the audience to see the matter through your eyes.

  • Be personal Tell them why is it important to solve the problem and point out instances of solved and unsolved situations.
  • Use illustrations Use visuals to arouse their interest and videos to illustrate the problem.
  • Give solutions Suggest some solutions as to how the situation can be solved and encourage them to voice their opinions too.
