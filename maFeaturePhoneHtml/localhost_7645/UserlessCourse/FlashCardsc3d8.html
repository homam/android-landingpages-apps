Attitudinal analysis

Attitudinal analysis takes into account the audience's attitudes, beliefs and values. It will help you understand the mindset of your audience and connect with them at a more intellectual and emotional level.

Examples of attitudinal analysis questions:
  1. What is the audience's attitude towards the topic?
  2. How are the audience's values and beliefs affected by this topic?
