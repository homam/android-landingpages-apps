Audience analysis

Audience analysis is the first step towards determining your audience and making your presentation based on the information you get.

If you went on a trip with your friends, the way you'd describe the trip to your mother would be different from the way you say it to your friends and then again, your spouse. That's why, it's always good to know as much as possible about your audience before you start your session, though we also know that it's not doable all the time.

We generally analyse our audience on three parameters:

  1. demographic analysis
  2. attitudinal analysis
  3. environmental analysis

You can also analyse your audience during and after the presentation through verbal and non-verbal cues and responses.
