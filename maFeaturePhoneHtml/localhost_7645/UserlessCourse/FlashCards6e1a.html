Where, when, what, how?

Consider when and where the negotiation will take place, the amount of time the negotiation will last, what parties will be involved and what the next step would be if the negotiation fails. Holding the meeting at your office gives you the psychological "home match" advantage. And you have easier access to any information you might need.

On the other hand, if you move the negotiation to your counterparty's place, you can commit yourself completely without any kind of disturbance.
