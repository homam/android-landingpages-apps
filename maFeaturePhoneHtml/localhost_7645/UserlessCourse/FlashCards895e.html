I made mistakes in drama. I thought drama was when actors cried. But drama is when the audience cries. Frank Capra 1897-1991

You will always face four categories of audience during your presentations - captives, pragmatists, socially motivated, and committed. In this chapter, we will give you the ins-and-outs of these four different types of audiences.
