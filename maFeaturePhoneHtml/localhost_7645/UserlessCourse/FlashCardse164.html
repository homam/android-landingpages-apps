Overview

If you want to advance in your company - or even just stick around and avoid getting laid off - you need to make yourself as valuable as possible to your employer. Even better, you want to try and make yourself irreplaceable. Granted, it could be argued that nobody is ever truly irreplaceable, but you want to come as close as possible. In this chapter there are some tools that will help you be the colleague no one wants to say goodbye to.
