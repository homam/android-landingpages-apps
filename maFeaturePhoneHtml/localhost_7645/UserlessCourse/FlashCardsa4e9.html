Show you're listening

People are afraid of speaking in public. According to research, public speaking is the biggest fear in the world. If you want your audience to speak, then show them that you are willing to listen to them. You can show this by your body language. Turn towards those who are speaking, nod your head in affirmation to what they are saying or lean a bit forward so that they feel that you are interested in whatever they are saying. These are non verbal ways of expressing that you care about what their perspective is.
