Compromise
They say it is better to be poor and happy than rich and miserable, but how about a compromise like moderately rich and just moody? Princess Diana 1961-1997

In her own way, Princess Diana is being pretty flexible here. But seriously, in order to tame your temper and getting your message across, showing flexibility is very important. Have a solution in mind but stay open to compromises. Try to keep listening and stay open to potential solutions, even if the arguments you're hearing appear to be completely irrational.

Word to the wise Don't agree to binding conditions or rules when you know you're not thinking straight.
