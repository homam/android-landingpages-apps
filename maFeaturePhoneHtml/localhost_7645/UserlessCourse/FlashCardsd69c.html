Ignite

Ignite calls itself 'a fast-paced geek event' and its slogan is "enlighten us but make it quick". That should give you some ideas as to what it actually is. At Ignite, you can speak on any topic of your choice for only five minutes. You bring 20 slides on your topic and they auto-advance every 15 seconds whether you're ready or not. Ignite runs events in over 100 cities around the world. For more information go to igniteshow.com
