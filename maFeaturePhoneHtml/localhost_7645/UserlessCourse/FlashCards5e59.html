Overview
How did Agamemnon anger Achilles? Answer: Achilles is so angry that he wants to kill Agamemnon because he had captured his woman, (who was captured in the first battle of Troy) used her for his own pleasure and then threw her to the Men like a piece of meat to the bulldogs.

You may find this a rather peculiar question starting this course and you even may not know who Achilles is. Let alone, Agamemnon. And because this is not a Greek mythology course, we will not go into this horrific story any deeper. What we do want to answer is the question: What is anger?
