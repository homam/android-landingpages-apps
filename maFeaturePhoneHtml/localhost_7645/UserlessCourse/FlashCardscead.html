Ethics

If people knew how hard I had to work to gain my mastery, it would not seem so wonderful at all Michelangelo 1475-1564

No matter what the job is, everyone who works at anything should always do their very best. Putting your best foot forward in all aspects of work is an essential key to success.

Good work ethics are:

  • Honesty and Integrity Being honest and demanding self-integrity will be noticed on the job. Employers seek and promote employees that own these qualities.
  • Dependability Those who are dependable are considered reliable. This is a great trait to be known for!
  • Efficiency Vital for your own growth as well as the betterment of the company. Inefficient employees will cost the organisation a lot of money.
  • Teamwork In every organisation you are part of a team. Act as a team-member which means that the greater good always comes first.
  • Positive attitude and initiative A positive attitude can inspire others around you. Taking the initiative to implement new ideas or better ways of doing things can demonstrate leadership
