Different strategies

Because of the gender differences, you have to plan different negotiation strategies. In general, women try to build up a relationship, are more compassionate and take a more non-aggressive stance. Men on the other hand, seek what they want to get out of the negotiation, not so worried about what will happen after the deal is done.

Did you know
  • That men start negotiations about four times as often as women.
  • When asked to describe the process of negotiation metaphorically, men pick: "winning a ballgame" and a "wrestling match", women pick: "going to the dentist".
  • Women are more pessimistic about how much they can get out of a negotiation and so typically ask for and get less when they negotiate - on average 30% less than men.
  • Many women accept what they are offered and don't negotiate their salaries.
  • Women often don't know their market value; they report salary expectations between 3 and 32% lower than those of men for the same jobs.
