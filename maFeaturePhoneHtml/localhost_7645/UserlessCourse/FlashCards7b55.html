Slide layout

Your slide layout determines the attention span of your audience. Consider the following points if you don't want your slides to look busy and distract the audience.

  • Don't use too many words per slide - just use keywords or a couple of phrases only.
  • Use numerical or bullet points instead of complete sentences.
  • Jot down a maximum of four to five points per slide for a maximum of one to two minutes.
  • Show one point at a time so that the audience remains focused on you and not on the slides.
  • Use animation with control and consistency.
