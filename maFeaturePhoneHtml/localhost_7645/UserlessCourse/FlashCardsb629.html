Blogging

Blogging can show an active interest in your industry. Comments on a companies' blog posts can result in them taking a look at yours. Blogging can be very influential. It can say a lot about your personality. More than if they would read your CV and cover letter.

Here some hints for putting together a memorable blog:

Choose a unique topic

This is going to be challenging. Since you want employers to be ' swept away' by the information you are communicating, you have to put extra effort in choosing a unique topic. For example, instead of blogging as interior designer, blog as the top designer in your town for young adults. Choose a niche.

Be professional

While many blogs discuss recipes or weird images, a professional blog should only have content that you want to share with your professional network. Comment for instance on an interesting article you found or review a book related to your profession. If you have a personal blog don't mix it with the professional one.

Write regularly

If you're going to create a blog, be committed. Establish a number of statuses you want to post per week. Keep it simple. Your posts can for instance be a link to an interesting article along with a few sentences why people should read this article.

Market your blog

Let the world know that you're blogging. Put your blog on your resume, list it with your personal information or put it under 'Relevant Experience.' Furthermore, you can also market your blog on other social media communities that you're using.
