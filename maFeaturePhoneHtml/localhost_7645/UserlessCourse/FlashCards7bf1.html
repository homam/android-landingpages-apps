Demographic analysis

Demographic analysis is the process where you identify your audience based on their age, gender, culture, ethnicity, race, religion and education level. You then adapt your presentation based on the gathered information. Take care that it appeals to all sections of your audience and doesn't offend anyone.

Examples of Demographic analysis questions:
  1. What is the age of the people in the audience?
  2. Are there any significant cultural differences among your audience?
  3. What is the ratio of men to women in the audience?

There are many ways to know what the audience feels at the end of your presentation, primarily through verbal and non-verbal responses. You can also conduct a survey and find out what the audience liked or disliked in your presentation.
