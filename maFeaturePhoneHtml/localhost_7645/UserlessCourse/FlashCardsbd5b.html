How to be disciplined

Here are some practical tools to get disciplined:

  • Create a daily schedule Create a time frame for each task. Don't surf the Internet or check your e-mails if it's not necessary.
  • Turn one task into many Break big tasks down into short tasks. Once you've finished one component, you'll gain momentum and enthusiasm.
  • Don't insist on perfection Perfection stands in the way of discipline. If you don't take any action before everything is totally perfect, you may end up delaying the process.
  • Reward yourself Reward yourself when you have completed a series of tasks. Take a short break so you can move on to the next ones. Self-discipline will be easier this way.
