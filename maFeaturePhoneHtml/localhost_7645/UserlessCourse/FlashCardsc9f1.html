Steve Jobs, the Visionary

Steve was among the greatest of American innovators — brave enough to think differently, bold enough to believe he could change the world, and talented enough to do it. President Obama 1961 -

Steve Jobs was one of the most successful visionary leaders of his time and a truly charismatic person. If you want to be a visionary leader, you have to be the idealist who’s constantly coming up with new initiatives. A visionary always moves forward with the organizational goals and enhance the overall objectives of the company.

These idealists have a clear vision in their mind and want to change the world into a better place.
