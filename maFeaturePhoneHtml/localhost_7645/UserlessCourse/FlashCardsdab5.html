External sources of anger

There are thousands of situations that can make us angry, but all these situations can be narrowed down into four general events:

  1. Personal attacks The other party attacks us personally in the form of verbal abuse.
  2. Attacks on ideas The other party shuts down our ideas and opinions.
  3. Attacks on needs The other party threatens to take away our basic needs.
  4. Frustration Our tolerance level for getting things done might be low or influenced by things happening in our lives.
