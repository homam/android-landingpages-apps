What causes the anger?

If you look closely at the root causes for anger in relationships, you will find out that the reason behind relationship drama has something to do with one or all of the following issues:

  • Feeling misunderstood In general, people do not expect everyone to completely understand them. They do expect this from their significant other. So, when this occurs, angry emotions come to the surface. Usually as a mask for a fear of not being loved.
  • Stress Stress is a relationship killer. It causes a person to become a time bomb.
  • Day to day things Day to day issues can become anger triggers. If your partner doesn't behave the way you want them to ("why won't you ever do the laundry?!"), you feel rejected or taken for granted. This type of anger often can be resolved or even be prevented if you communicate about your feelings.
