Backup

You have created a wonderful presentation replete with PowerPoint slides, audio and video clips. However, when you reach the podium you realise that the projector doesn't work, the connection is lost and technology has failed you. What do you do? You don't even have time to panic because, as they say, the show must go on.

That's why you should always keep a backup of your handwritten notes and presentation outline.

Keep a back up of your presentation on a USB and have a print out copy as well.
