Spelling and grammar

Be aware! Your presentation will reflect badly on you if you use incorrect spelling and grammar. Always proof-read your slides multiple times before your presentation. Check for spelling mistakes, use of repetitive words and any grammatical errors. If you're not a native user of the language you are presenting in, have someone else with the language proficiency check your presentation.
