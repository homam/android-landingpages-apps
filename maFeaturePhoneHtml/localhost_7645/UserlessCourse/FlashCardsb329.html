Margarine

Margarine is rich in omega-6 fatty acids, the ones that block out the mood-enhancing, brain-building omega-3 fatty acids.

Not only do these acids fight the good ones - which results in you feeling down and irritated - they are also known to be bad for your health and can lead to high cholesterol levels, insulin resistance, oxidation, inflammation and heart problems.

Alternative Try using butter instead of margarine. It is a healthier alternative. Olive oil is great for cooking because it contains healthy unsaturated fat.
