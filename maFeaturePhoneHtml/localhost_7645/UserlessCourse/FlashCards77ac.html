Initiate a pause

When you feel pressurised or under stress you can ask for a pause. You might prevent yourself from saying things you may later regret. It also provides a moment of reflection. This gives you the time to think about your reactions so that you don't close the deal too quickly and regret about it later. How to initiate a pause?

  • If the negotiation gets complicated and you do not want to get further into the matter, ask for a night to think the negotiation over. Most people will respect your request to 'sleep on it'.
  • Excuse yourself to go to the restroom. Of course this will never be a problem.
  • In a business situation you can always say that you have to run it by your partner and get back to you the next morning.
  • Ask for a drink break. This will give you time to think over the matter.
  • If the other party is proposing something you disagree on, ask him to repeat it and write it down. This process almost always causes the counterpart to consider his words and adjust his message.
  • Ask for a clarification on some topic that has already been covered.
