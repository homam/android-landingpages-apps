Shop around

Unless you're an experienced home renovator, it's going to be pretty difficult for you to get a good quote for your next home improvement project. You don't have any comparable quotations and don't know how to proceed. The first thing that you should do, is to get at least three quotations for the same work from three different contractors.

You'll find many contractors are willing to reduce their quotes or re-negotiate the contracts to get the work. In the process, you get a good deal.

Must do Provide each of the contractors with as much information as possible for absolute transparency and complete pricing.
