8. Don't get physical

Hitting your child if they have done something you think is naughty, simply doesn't work. In many countries it is also illegal. Your child will copy the way you act. They may turn into bullies at school, and even hit back at you next time. Never ever hit or harm your child. If you are about to snap, count to 10. It may calm the whole situation down. If you suspect you cannot control those impulses seek professional help.
