How to take up more responsibilities
  • Think! before you ask, think about what responsibilities you will be able to perform well. Going beyond your depth may have the opposite effect you are looking for.
  • Check! Be ready and prepared to handle the extra work. Investigate how much time this extra work will actually take.
  • Be honest! Your boss may not know how much you already have on your plate, so when he asks you to do more, it is perfectly acceptable to decline extra responsibility. Offer a different time frame or try to work out a way it can be handled within your current workload.
