Be empathetic

Empathy is the ability to step into the shoes of another person, aiming to understand their feelings and perspectives in order to use that understanding to guide our actions. In general, empathetic people are happier than the more self absorbed ones.

For the ones who are not (that) empathetic we have good news: you can make empathy an attitude and part of your daily life and thus improve the life of everyone around you. Start by reading these tools:

  • Understand your own emotions What kind of situations change your emotion? Use this info as a point from which to understand others' personal emotions.
  • Spend time with different people Get to know people of all ages, socio-economic backgrounds, ethnicities and levels of physical and mental ability.
  • Seek similarities When we emphasise differences between ourselves and other people it is difficult to understand others. So for greater empathy, seek out similarities between you and others.
  • Practice seeing things through another person's eyes - Train your mind to be open to perspectives other than your own.
