Tips on making your Facebook Profile Page
  1. First, make a decision whether to keep Facebook for personal social reasons or for some professional use.
  2. If you want to use Facebook for professional purposes, take a critical look at your profile and decide what you want your professional network to see.
  3. Create a simple profile.
  4. Weigh the pros and cons if you're thinking about a 'colourful' post and save the gossip for your personal friends
  5. Don't spam the audience with your photos.
  6. Post relevant information linked to your job search.
  7. Choose your Friends wisely. Everybody can see your network.
