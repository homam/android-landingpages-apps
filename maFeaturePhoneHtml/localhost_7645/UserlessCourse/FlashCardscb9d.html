Pragmatists

The pragmatists are partially interested when they arrive for your presentation. They don't care much about theory and look for the practical utilisation of the topic. Pragmatists are:

  1. always brimming with ideas
  2. keen to test these ideas in practice
  3. task-oriented
  4. are quick to accept and equally swift to reject anything that doesn't have an obvious application in their work
