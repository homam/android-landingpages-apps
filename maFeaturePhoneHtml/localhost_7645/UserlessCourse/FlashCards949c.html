Video recording

To take videos, look for the slider bar at the right bottom corner of the screen. You will see two icons: one looks like a still camera and the other looks like a video. It has a square with a triangle coming out. Slide the bar towards the video and start recording by tapping on the red button with which you take pictures. A timer will appear when the recording starts. To stop the recording tap the red button again.
