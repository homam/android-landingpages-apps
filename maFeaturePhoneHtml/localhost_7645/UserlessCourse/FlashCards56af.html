Boss-bonding tips
  • Give a helping hand Always ask if you can help in any way to make her current project successful.
  • Be honest If you think you won't be able to accomplish something in time, be honest about it and tell him when you can finish the task.
  • Try to build a rapport Once you're at the same pace, you will be able to perform better.
  • Flattery A person who gets positive comments from you will generally think highly of you too. Be sincere though.
