Recognise anger triggers

You can learn to detect the signals that tell you that anger is about to spiral out of control. First you have to know what these signals are. You can see/feel someone:

  • becoming tense
  • breathing more rapidly
  • getting red in the face
  • snapping (verbally)
  • becoming sarcastic
  • withdrawing from you
  • blowing up over little things
  • ignoring requests
