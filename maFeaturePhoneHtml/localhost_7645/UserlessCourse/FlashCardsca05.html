Overview

Good preparation is the key to all negotiations. Whether you have a business or personal negotiation, make sure you have done your 'homework' to achieve your goals. Going into a negotiation well prepared can make all the difference in feeling satisfied with the final outcome.

Doing your homework for negotiations can mean several things. It can include making a list of what you want to achieve and what is important to you as well as trying to understand what is important to the other person you are negotiating with.
