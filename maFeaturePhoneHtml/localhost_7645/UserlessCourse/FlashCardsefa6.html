Interior lighting

Turn on all the lights in the room. Take note of the room windows if there are any. The light coming through them can affect your picture. Adjust your exposure accordingly as the windows can become too bright and the room too dark. Standard tungsten bulbs cast an effective light. Another trick to exclude bright daylight - pull the curtains!

Tip

Tip

Tip
Do not use your flash. Try taking the picture in natural light. Your exposure will be long but that's not a problem if you put your camera on a tripod.
