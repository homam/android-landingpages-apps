Career goals

Career goals are simply the things that you want to achieve in your career. Clearly defined, they can help you move towards those goals more quickly.

Ask yourself:
  • Where do you want to be five years from now?
  • Why?
  • How can you get there?
  • What additional skills or training do you need to reach there?

Take the time to think through these questions. Write them down to help with the process.
