Challenging goals

It is important to strike a balance between goals that are too low and those that are too high. If your goals are too low then you have missed an opportunity to do better. If they are too high, you risk feeling frustrated as they may be unattainable. Try to set realistic goals.

Know that you have other options

Whether the object of your affection is a stock, a piece of real estate, or a potential partner, you must have in mind that there are always other options. Practice the mantra: "There is always another option around the corner."

Know the other options

Don't develop just one alternative deal in a negotiation. Make a list of all possible alternatives. What are your options if you walk away from this deal? You have nothing to lose by listing your choices.

Work out the options

After you have created the list of alternatives, decide which one is most acceptable to you. Decide what you want to do first if the negotiation doesn't close. Play that scenario out in your mind.
