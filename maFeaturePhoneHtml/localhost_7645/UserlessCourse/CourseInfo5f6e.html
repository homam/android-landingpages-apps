A boss creates fear, a leader confidence. A boss fixes blame, a leader corrects mistakes. A boss knows all, a leader asks questions. A boss makes work drudgery, a leader makes it interesting. A boss is interested in themselves, a leader is interested in the group. Russell Ewing 1885-1976

Leadership helps a nation pull through in tough times, it makes a failing business the greatest thing ever, it helps others see things in ways they didn't see it before, it empowers young minds to achieve the impossible. If you have the willpower and desire to lead, you can become a leader.

Leadership is the process by which one person is able to empower, inspire and motivate another person or group of people in achieving their goals. A leader helps us see what we can't. He shows the path to follow and has the ability to visualize what we can achieve.

Leaders are not necessarily born as one; they can be molded, trained and improvised. Do you have what it takes to be a leader? Do you want to help other people in achieving their goals? Do you want to change the thought process of your peer group? Do you want to climb the ladder of promotions at your workplace? Do you want to be a leader?

Then, let's get started!

* In this course we are referring to a leader as a 'he'. Of course the he-word can easily be replaced by 'she'.
