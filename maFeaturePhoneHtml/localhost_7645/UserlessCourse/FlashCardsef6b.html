Responding

It is better not to respond to online anger, but in some situations a response is needed.

In all cases, your response should be based on facts, not on emotions.

Remember: Your response should be a peaceful, non-hostile message. You don't want to fight fire with fire... If you are unable to respond in a polite manner, it is advisable not to confront the online aggressor.
