Be cool and collective

Your tone of voice is crucial. Try not to yell and act over-emotional. Express the fact that you care for the other person and allow them to show their feelings towards you. This will help both parties to remain civilised. You may have a tendency to let the argument escalate and become hysterical. Recognising this pattern is vital. The trick is to break this pattern and to make a different choice. Consider all the benefits:

  • More chance of being listened to and understood.
  • Less chance of feeling ashamed or guilty afterwards.
  • Less pressure on the relationship or friendship.
