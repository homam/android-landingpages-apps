1. Stay calm

The best way to calm down is to take 10 deep breaths. Remove yourself from the object of your frustration and take deep breaths until you calm down. Scream in a pillow if you want to, but do not try to settle any situation with your child while you're angry. This video will give you some practical exercises to calm your body to restore your coolness again.
