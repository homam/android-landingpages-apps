Types of thinking

Good leaders think ahead and see problems before any one else sees them so they can prevent them from happening. Good leaders also see potential opportunities that will benefit the company and employees.

Strategic leadership requires five different types of thinking.

  1. Critical thinking is objectively analysing a situation making use of the information from all possible sources.
  2. Action driven thinking is the ability to organise ideas in such a practical way that they immediately can be put into practice.
  3. Conceptual thinking is the ability to find connections between abstract ideas and then make a complete plan out of them.
  4. Innovative thinking is all about generating new ideas or new ways of handling and approaching things.
  5. Intuitive thinking is the ability to, without knowledge, let your intuition play a great part in setting out your plans.
