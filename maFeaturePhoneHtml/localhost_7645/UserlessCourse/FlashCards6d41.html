Financial

What are your financial goals? Often people will give a general answer, such as achieving financial security. You increase your ability to achieve your financial goals when you are more specific. Try answering the next few questions to help you.

Ask yourself :
  • Where are you currently at financially? Are your expenses in line with your income? Do you have debt? What gaps do you need to fill?
  • Is there something you would like to have but need to save for?
  • How do your financial goals affect others?
  • What do you need to plan for savings or emergency funds?
  • What expenses do you anticipate in the future? Do your goals match up with those costs?
