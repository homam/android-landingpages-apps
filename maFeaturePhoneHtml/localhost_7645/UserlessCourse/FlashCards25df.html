Blur the background

Can you make stunning candid portraits or still life photos when the background is blur and the subject is in perfect focus? Yes you can. Some mobile phones have the option of 'image destabilisation' and can be set to achieve some amount of background blur. The lens and the sensor move together with the subject in complete focus and thus blur the background to some extent. Even if your phone doesn't have this setting, you can still take a photo with a blurred background. Follow these steps:

  1. Keep your subject far from the background
  2. Come closer to the subject and then zoom in
  3. Choose portrait mode
  4. Take your photo

There are useful apps on the market which help you create a blurry background. Check out Big Lens and After Focus.
