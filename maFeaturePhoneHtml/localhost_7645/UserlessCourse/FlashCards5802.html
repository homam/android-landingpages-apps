Spinach

We know you're not Popeye and we know that veggies aren't necessarily the first thing you have in mind when you're thinking about happy food. You should, because spinach is rich in folate, a natural B-vitamin that helps the brain create serotonin which is a neurotransmitter sending happy signals to your brain.

Did you know that leafy vegetables such as spinach contain more vitamins and minerals and less calories than other vegetables.
