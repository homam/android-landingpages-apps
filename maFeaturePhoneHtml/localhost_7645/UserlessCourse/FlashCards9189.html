Overview

What makes a great leader? Some say it's charisma, others say it is his heroic personality and some say it's the strategy he follows. But these are just qualities that he possesses, a leader becomes great when he has a strong team who follows or imitates him, believes in him and gets inspired by him.

Leaders should know how to build a team from a group of people belonging to different backgrounds. They know how to lead team-building activities, help team members in understanding the team process and gather information from different sources.

In this chapter, you will learn about different team types, how a leader builds one and what needs to be taken under consideration to build a team.
