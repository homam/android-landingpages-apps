Focus

What happens when we lose our focus? There can be many reasons for our waning interest, such as not seeing visible progress as quickly as we would like. So we lose sight of our goals. Here are some helpful tips to refuel your fire:

Be clear about what you want

Write your goal in detail. How will your goal improve your life and make you feel more fulfilled. Then make sure to read this every day. Keep that vision STRONG in your mind.

Why you want it

Write out your wish and follow up with deeper questions. For example: "Within two years I want to lead this department. This will make me feel better about myself." The next question would be: "Why do I want to feel better about myself?" "I want to feel better about myself because then I will be a happier person." Ah.. so here, the TRUE goal is to be a happier person. Can I do that without becoming head of the department? Sure. But the key isn't to talk yourself out of your goals. Understand what your TRUE goals are. Once you understand what you really want, you will be much more focused in your efforts to achieve it.

Set a plan of action

It is difficult to reach your goals without a plan of action. This action plan will be a critical tool in enabling you to take steps towards achieving those goals. Having milestones and deadlines will motivate you to move ahead in stages and gives you opportunities to evaluate your goals and the plan from time to time.Think about the outcome you're pursuing, and then think about each step you will have to take to reach your goals.

Review and Revise

One mistake many of us make is that we set our plans and never look at them again. But often plans need to be revised to keep them actual and relevant.

Believe

Act like it's already your reality. The creation process begins in the MIND. If you can envision your goals then you are that much closer to achieving them.
