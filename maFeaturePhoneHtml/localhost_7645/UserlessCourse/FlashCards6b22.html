Megapixels (MP)

A Megapixel (MP) is one million pixels. A pixel is a picture element. A digital picture is made of millions of tiny coloured dots. The more pixels the less grainy a picture will be. More pixels will also give you higher quality enlargements.

A camera phone sensor converts light into electricity and then converts the same electricity into a digital image. This image is stored on the data card. At least one or two megapixels of sensor resolution is required to produce a decent 4x6 print. But even a 3.2 MP camera is capable of creating good detailed images.
