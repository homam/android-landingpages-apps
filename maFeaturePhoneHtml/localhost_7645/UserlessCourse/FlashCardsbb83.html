Take detailed notes

It's important to take down notes, be it in the form of key words, phrases or sentences. They prove to be very useful on the negotiation table and can help you get what you want.

Draft an email to your counterpart shortly after your conversation. Request for confirmation thus ensuring that both of you have the same understanding.

Don't be too focussed on taking notes. This can prevent you from taking close attention. Writing down your notes soon after is advisable.
