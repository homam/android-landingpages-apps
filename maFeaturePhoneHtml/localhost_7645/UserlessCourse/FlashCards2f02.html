Be a professional

Earn a reputation for being professional, dependable and a cooperative person. Act and look accordingly.

Some do's:

  • Ask questions when you are insecure
  • Dare to be different - stand out from the pack.
  • Keep a positive outlook on things
  • Don't whine or complain or blame others
  • Don't be a clock-watcher.
  • Be a problem-solver. Don't go to your boss with problems and if you have to: be sure to come up with at least one solution
